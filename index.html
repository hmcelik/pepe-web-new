The Project

Memecraft has arrived! Dive into this immersive memecoin adventure with endless potential. Travel around the famous town of Pepeville, and play as the meme mayor Pepe himself. Step into the role of the main character in the crypto meme universe and chart your own course!

This is just the beginning. Uncover easter eggs and engage in competitions with SOL rewards, within the pixel RPG adventure. Our vision includes developing a groundbreaking 3D Pepe game.

Whether a fan of $MOG, $SLERF, want to do side quests with ansem or just have a fondness for drogs (same here) you've found the perfect match!
